Details

Locate the x-y coordinate of candles formed from 8pm to 9pm (Asia/Manila timezone). Find the highest and lowest points to create a range rectangle:

┌────────────── sessionHigh
│
│ ████ ← 8PM–9PM candles
│
└────────────── sessionLow

Mark the high, low, and midpoint of the range:

┌────────────── HIGH
│ ████
│ ████
────────────── MID
│ ████
│ ████
└────────────── LOW

The high is used as the Buy Stop. TP is calculated as: TP = High + 2 × (High − Mid). The low is used as the Sell Stop with mirrored logic. The midpoint serves as SL for both orders.

Sample Framework

Regardless of the colors, the output is well presented from the input:

Breakdown

  • 8pm to 9pm Range – Define the price range during this hour.
  • Buy Stop – Place at the high of the range.
    • SL: Midpoint of the range
    • TP: High + 2×R
  • Sell Stop – Place at the low of the range.
    • SL: Midpoint of the range
    • TP: Low − 2×R
  • Maximum of 2 orders may trigger per setup.
  • Maximum of 4 outcomes: two losses and two wins.
  • Setup is valid until 4am.
